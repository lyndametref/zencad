Other operations.

This section lists operations that do not fit any other section.

unify

This operation simplifies the geometric representation of an object by removing unnecessary edges and face joins.

Can be performed on 2D and 3D objects.

Signature: 

unify(shp)

Example:

unify(cylinder(r=10, h=10) + cylinder(r=10, h=10).move(5,5))
