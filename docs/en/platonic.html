Платоновы тела

Construction of Platonic solids. The library is based on https: github.comqalle2plato.scad

Correct polyhedron Number of vertices Number of edges Number of faces Number of sides at a face Number of edges adjacent to a vertex Type of spatial symmetry
Tetrahedron 4 6 4 3 3 Td
Hexahedron 8 12 6 4 3 Oh
Octahedron 6 12 8 3 4 Oh
Dodecaidr 20 30 12 5 3 Ih
Icosahedron 12 30 20 3 5 Ih

The library allows you to specify the dimensions of bodies through the radius of the circumscribed circle r or through the length of the edge a .

Signatures: 

zencad.platonic.tetrahedron(r=1, a=None, shell=False)
zencad.platonic.hexahedron(r=1, a=None, shell=False)
zencad.platonic.octahedron(r=1, a=None, shell=False)
zencad.platonic.dodecahedron(r=1, a=None, shell=False)
zencad.platonic.icosahedron(r=1, a=None, shell=False)

# Alternative syntax
zencad.platonic.platonic(nfaces, r=1, a=None, shell=False)

Example:

# Через радиус:
tetrahedron(10)
hexahedron(10)
octahedron(r=10)
dodecahedron(r=10)
icosahedron(10)

# Through the length of the rib:
icosahedron(a=10)

# Alternative syntax:
zencad.platonic.platonic(4, 10)
zencad.platonic.platonic(6, 10)
zencad.platonic.platonic(8, 10)
zencad.platonic.platonic(12, 10)
zencad.platonic.platonic(20, 10)
